Caramel

20 mins prep time, 30 minutes cook time. One medium jar, about 200 spoonfulls

Ingredients

  • 350g white sugar
  • 1 stick unsalted butter, chopped into small cubes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbs fine sea salt

Instructions

WARNING: Melted sugar can be extremly dangerous on skin as it is still sticky and viscous at high temperature

  1. Place sugar into pan (Must not be non-stick), and set flame to high
  2. Stir consistently until all sugar melts, to prevent burning
  3. Reduce heat to low
  4. Once sugar is fully melted slowly mix in butter, be careful as the mix will quickly bubble up
  5. Constatnly whisk to make sure sugar and butter do not separate
  6. Gradually and stir heavily while adding cream since this will cause the mix to bubble and steam to risk of overflow
  7. Once all cream has been added stop stirring, raise heat to medium and let the mix boil for 1 minute at 220F
  8. Remove from heat and stir in salt, then quickly transfer to container before the caramel cools and thickens

Notes:

Can be stored in refigerator for up to one month. Will solidify, microwave to reach desired consitency.
