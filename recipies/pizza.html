Pizza Dough

Ingredient List

  • 700g all purpose flour
  • 80ml virgin olive oil
  • 50g yeast
  • 1 tbs fine sea salt
  • 1 cup water
  • Plastic wrap

Instructions

  1. First mix flour and water in a large bowl
  2. Knead until no longer sticky
  4. Add olive oil
  5. Place on counter top, add yeast and knead until it passes the window test
  6. Place back in bowl and seal with plastic wrap
  7. Let rise to double volume
  8. Now is a good time to preheat oven to 450F
  9. Roll out into desired shape and form crust
  10. Add toppings and bake at 450F for 8 minutes
