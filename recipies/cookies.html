Chocolate Chip Cookies

20 mins prep time, 15 minutes bake time. Roughly 2 dozen cookies

Ingredient List

  • 650g all purpose flour
  • 200g white sugar
  • 50g brown sugar
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1/4 tbs salt
  • 1/2 tbs baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Get a bowl, then place white sugar, salt, and baking powder
  2. Get a microwave save bowl, place butter inside then carefully microwave unitl fully melted
  3. Quickly add brown sugar and eggs into bowl and stir until fully combined
  4. Combine both bowls
  5. Now is a good time to preheat the oven to 350F
  6. Gradually mix in sifted flour
  7. Gradually add in chocolate chips
  8. Get a baking sheet and place parchment paper on top
  9. Scoop out balls of dough, ideally 1 1/4 inch diameter
  10. Bake in oven at 350F for 12 minutes
